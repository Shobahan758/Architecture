How natural light changes a floor plan

Natural light is not decoration. It changes circulation, privacy, comfort, and how materials behave across the day. A strong plan studies where light enters, where it pauses, and how each room can use that rhythm without glare or waste.

For clients, daylight planning also makes decisions easier. Furniture, storage, wall finishes, and artwork all become clearer when the room has a known relationship to morning, afternoon, and evening light.

Good architecture makes time visible without making the room feel busy.
